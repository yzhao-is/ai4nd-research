# ai4nd-research

Research materials for a study on how neurodivergent adults use AI chatbots to navigate everyday social situations.

## About This Study

This research explores how neurodivergent adults use AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or other chatbots to navigate social situations — whether that's drafting a message, figuring out what someone meant, preparing for a difficult conversation, or working through social expectations.
**Positionality:** This study is led by a neurodivergent researcher.

## Who Can Participate

- 18 years or older
- Self-identify as neurodivergent (e.g., autism, ADHD, dyslexia, dyscalculia, dyspraxia, OCD, Tourette syndrome — no formal diagnosis required)
- Use AI chatbots for everyday communication

## What's Involved

1. **Diary Tool** — Log a few examples of how you use AI chatbots for social/interpersonal communication using our diary tool (see below).
2. **Interview** — One follow-up interview (~45 min, via Zoom or text-based chat).
3. **Compensation** — $20 gift card as a thank-you.
